Amare

A fully custom creative subscription platform designed and built from scratch for a client.

Amare combines a branded digital experience with a physical mail concept, allowing the project to present curated monthly content and creative material in a refined and visually cohesive way.

The platform was developed with a strong focus on visual storytelling, brand consistency, and a smooth user experience, ensuring that the digital presence reflects the tone and aesthetic of the project.

Project Overview

Amare was commissioned as a bespoke client platform to support a creative mail subscription concept. The goal was to build a site that could communicate the value of the subscription clearly while maintaining a soft, artistic visual language.

The project required a fully custom structure rather than a template-based solution, allowing the design and experience to be tailored specifically to the brand.

What brought the project to life

The client needed more than a simple landing page. The project required a digital presence that could explain the subscription clearly, feel aligned with the creative direction of the brand, and present the experience in a way that felt thoughtful rather than transactional.

That meant shaping the structure, content flow and visual treatment around the concept itself, so the website supported the tone of the brand instead of feeling like a generic template adapted after the fact.

What the client was really asking for

A refined client-facing platform that could carry the feeling of the brand, communicate the subscription offer properly, and give visitors confidence in the overall concept from the first impression onward.

What Was Delivered

Custom work shaped around brand identity, user experience and launch readiness.

  • Custom front-end interface designed around the brand identity
  • Responsive layout optimized for both desktop and mobile
  • Structured product presentation for subscription tiers
  • Visual storytelling through layout and content flow
  • Clean, lightweight architecture designed for long-term use

Delivery

Custom system designed and deployed for a client

Built as a bespoke client project rather than adapted from a generic template, with the digital experience shaped around the creative direction of the brand.

Who It Was For

Built around a premium creative brand

Designed for a client that needed a more distinctive subscription presentation, clearer storytelling, and a polished visual experience aligned with the brand.

Why It Matters

Proof of tailored delivery

Amare shows that the studio can build something visually specific, brand-sensitive, and deployment-ready without relying on a generic template feel.

Design Direction

A soft, premium digital experience shaped around storytelling and presentation.

The visual system was intended to feel thoughtful, calm and curated, helping the platform communicate creative value rather than feeling like a generic ecommerce build.

Visual approach

Layout, spacing and content flow were used to support a more editorial presentation style, giving the subscription concept a stronger sense of identity.

Experience approach

The interaction model was kept lightweight and clear so the brand experience remained central instead of getting buried under unnecessary interface complexity.

Business Goal

Communicate the subscription clearly

The platform needed to explain the concept, build trust in the offer, and present the subscription in a way that felt premium and easy to understand.

Studio Value

Tailored structure over generic templates

This project shows the value of shaping the structure around the product and brand, rather than trying to force a more sensitive concept into a standard website pattern.

Outcome

Polished and client-facing

The result is a polished client-facing platform that presents the subscription concept clearly while supporting the creative direction of the brand.

The project demonstrates the studio's ability to design and build tailored digital platforms where brand experience is central to the product.
