Amare

A bespoke subscription platform built for a premium creative client experience.

Amare combines digital membership access, recurring subscriptions, and physical monthly fulfillment inside one cohesive product journey.

From onboarding and checkout to archive access and admin operations, the platform was designed to convert cleanly and run reliably at launch.

Project Overview

Amare was commissioned to launch a premium subscription concept where digital content and physical delivery needed to feel part of one brand world.

The project required a fully custom build rather than a template setup, so the product flow, tone, and conversion journey could match the client brand precisely.

What brought the project to life

The client needed more than a simple landing page. They needed a product experience that communicated value quickly, built trust, and supported ongoing monthly operations.

That meant shaping structure, content flow, and conversion points around the offer itself so the platform could feel premium while remaining commercially clear.

What the client was really asking for

A refined subscription platform that could carry the brand tone, explain plans clearly, and provide a reliable base for recurring growth.

What Was Delivered

Custom work shaped around premium UX, subscription conversion, and launch readiness.

  • Custom brand-led interface with a soft premium visual system
  • Responsive subscription journey optimized across mobile and desktop
  • Tiered plan presentation with clear pricing and decision flow
  • Integrated customer accounts, archive access, and purchase flow
  • Admin and operations tooling to support ongoing monthly delivery

Delivery

End-to-end platform delivery for a live client

Delivered as a bespoke product build, not a template adaptation, with design and engineering aligned to both brand experience and business execution.

Who It Was For

Built around a premium subscription concept

Designed for a client that needed stronger offer clarity, a distinctive brand experience, and a platform-ready structure for recurring engagement.

Why It Matters

Proof of commercial + brand alignment

Amare shows the studio can deliver platforms that look premium, communicate value clearly, and support real operational workflows after launch.

Design Direction

A soft, premium digital experience shaped around storytelling and presentation.

The visual system was intended to feel thoughtful, calm and curated, helping the platform communicate creative value rather than feeling like a generic ecommerce build.

Visual approach

Layout, spacing and content flow were used to support a more editorial presentation style, giving the subscription concept a stronger sense of identity.

Experience approach

The interaction model was kept lightweight and clear so the brand experience remained central instead of getting buried under unnecessary interface complexity.

Business Goal

Communicate the subscription clearly

The platform needed to explain the concept, build trust in the offer, and present the subscription in a way that felt premium and easy to understand.

Studio Value

Tailored structure over generic templates

This project shows the value of shaping the structure around the product and brand, rather than trying to force a more sensitive concept into a standard website pattern.

Outcome

Polished and client-facing

The result is a polished client-facing platform that communicates the offer clearly and supports a repeatable subscription business model.

The project demonstrates Empire Build Solutions' ability to build tailored platforms where brand experience and commercial performance are both priorities.
