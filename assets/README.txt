Put your website assets here.

Recommended structure:

- assets/logos/
  Main brand logo files, icon marks, SVG exports, transparent PNGs

- assets/static/
  General site images, backgrounds, textures, icons, and reusable graphics

- assets/projects/amare/
  Amare screenshots, mockups, product visuals, and promo images

- assets/projects/bookinoo/
  Bookinoo screenshots, dashboards, mobile views, and feature visuals

- assets/projects/general/
  Other project screenshots, case study images, and future work previews
