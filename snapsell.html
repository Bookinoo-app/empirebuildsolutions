What It Is

SnapSell is a speed-first storefront platform built for normal business owners.

The product promise is simple: create an account, choose what you sell, pick a style, brand the store, add a product, and publish fast.

The experience is designed to feel closer to a launch flow than a traditional website builder.

Why it is a sale opportunity

SnapSell already has a meaningful MVP foundation, which makes it attractive as a commerce product base that can be acquired, branded further, customized for a niche, or extended into a larger storefront platform.

Product Vision

A guided storefront launch flow for business owners who want speed, clarity and simplicity.

SnapSell is designed to feel simpler than a traditional builder by helping the user move through setup in a more direct sequence: account, store type, style, branding, product, launch.
