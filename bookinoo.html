Bookinoo

Bookinoo is a fully deployed booking, communication and operations platform for service businesses that run on appointments and live customer messaging.

Production deployment went live on 23/05/2026 across active nodes: Barber-family, Mechanic, Restaurant, and Clinic.

Core capabilities include multi-channel customer intake, policy-aware booking automation, in-flow payments, QR attendance, and restaurant driver-dispatch operations.

What It Is

Bookinoo is an operational platform built for appointment-based services and live customer channels. It reduces manual admin, improves communication and centralizes daily execution.

The product is fully deployed and commercially positioned for acquisition, licensing, customization, or equity participation under Empire Build Solutions Ltd.

What the product is solving

Many service businesses still run core operations through fragmented messaging, manual calendars, reminders and disconnected tools. That creates friction around booking control, visibility, and follow-up.

Bookinoo addresses this in a live system by unifying intake, booking policy, payment flow and operational state tracking into one platform.

Why it is a commercial opportunity

Instead of starting from zero, a buyer or strategic partner can use Bookinoo as a deployed foundation with working capability depth, then adapt it to a target market and growth model.
