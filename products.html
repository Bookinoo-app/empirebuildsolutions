Amare

A fully custom creative subscription platform designed and built from scratch for a client.

Amare combines a branded digital experience with a physical mail concept, allowing the project to present curated monthly content and creative material in a refined and visually cohesive way.

The platform was developed with a strong focus on visual storytelling, brand consistency, and a smooth user experience, ensuring that the digital presence reflects the tone and aesthetic of the project.

DeliveryCustom system designed and deployed for a client.
FocusCreative platform design and brand-driven user experience.

Project Overview

Amare was commissioned as a bespoke client platform to support a creative mail subscription concept. The goal was to build a site that could communicate the value of the subscription clearly while maintaining a soft, artistic visual language.

The project required a fully custom structure rather than a template-based solution, allowing the design and experience to be tailored specifically to the brand.

Key Elements Delivered

  • Custom front-end interface designed around the brand identity
  • Responsive layout optimized for both desktop and mobile
  • Structured product presentation for subscription tiers
  • Visual storytelling through layout and content flow
  • Clean, lightweight architecture designed for long-term use

Outcome

The result is a polished client-facing platform that presents the subscription concept clearly while supporting the creative direction of the brand.

The project demonstrates the studio's ability to design and build tailored digital platforms where brand experience is central to the product.

Bookinoo

Bookinoo is a booking and client communication platform designed for service businesses that rely on appointments, scheduling and direct customer interaction.

The platform is being developed as a flexible operational foundation that can be customized for different industries such as salons, barbershops, mechanics, consultants or small service teams.

Rather than relying on fragmented tools for scheduling, reminders and client communication, Bookinoo aims to bring these workflows into one structured platform.

Use CaseAppointment-driven service businesses.
OpportunityAcquire the platform, customize it, or continue development toward a full product launch.

Product Vision

Many small and medium service businesses rely on disconnected systems - messaging apps, manual calendars, spreadsheets and reminders - to manage daily operations.

Bookinoo is designed to centralize these workflows into a single platform that simplifies scheduling, communication and operational oversight.

Platform Capabilities

  • Appointment scheduling and booking management
  • Client communication and follow-up workflows
  • Centralized operational overview
  • Scalable structure for additional automation features
  • Flexible architecture for customization and extension

Ideal Use Cases

  • Appointment-based service businesses
  • Operators managing high volumes of bookings
  • Businesses seeking to reduce manual scheduling work
  • Founders looking to launch a service-focused SaaS product

Opportunity

Bookinoo is currently in active development and available for acquisition, customization or continued product development.

The platform provides a structured foundation that can be adapted into a fully deployed product for a specific industry or business model.
